About me

As a Collective Intelligence Specialist, I specialize in leveraging the power of collective wisdom for effective decision-making through crowdsourcing, collaborative decision-making, and social network analysis.

My expertise extends to integrating Artificial Intelligence into Collective Intelligence frameworks. Proficient in web development, I implement user-friendly platforms for collective input which I aggregate and analyze data using ML algorithms and NLP techniques.

My passion lies in the intersection of collective management, leadership, and innovative governance, particularly in sectors such as healthcare and social sciences.

If you believe my profile aligns with the goals of your project, I welcome the opportunity to connect and discuss potential collaborations. Feel free to reach out.
